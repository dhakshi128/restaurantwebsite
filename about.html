Founder

Founder Hercules born in the year of 1945 in the city named 'Madurai'.From birth he was the one who was so much interested towards cooking. He always used to assist his mother in the kitchen.He used to share the dishes with his friends in the school and college.And he pursued a Degree in Engineering.

All of a sudden he and his family meet with the financial crisis. At this point, He with his mother started a road-side shop where they use to sell the dishes especially that of fish.

Restaurant

Hercules found it be fascinating and wanted to convert the selling of their dishes into a hotel or a restaurant.And they found that their dishes where selling to the peak and thereby started a hotel and hence became one of the top running restaurants in India by 1995 and still holding the position.

Motto of Hercules was always to provide people with tasty as well as healthy food.Hence the reason of the success is making customer's satisfaction as their first priority
